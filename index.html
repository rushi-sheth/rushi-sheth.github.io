About Me

Who Am I?

I am a recent graduate from Lehigh Univeristy with a Masters in Industrial Engineering(May'19). After completing my Bachelors degree in Mechanical Engineering, I found managing and improving processes more interesting than designing and manufacturing machines. While I started my Master's with no defined focus track, I eventually pursued the Data Science track. I believe this was a result of my prior classes in computer science during the high school, curiosity for problem solving and algortihms and the joy of witnessing direct impact of DS on business improvement.

What did these two degrees teach me?

It taught me how to Imagine, Create, Execute, Optimize.
I can not only make things, but can also improve it!
